How to Use This Timeline

  • Scroll horizontally to explore different time periods (1925-2025)
  • Click theme buttons to filter and highlight related movies and events
  • Hover over movies (blue rectangles) to see details
  • Hover over events (colored dots) to see how they connect to sci-fi themes
  • Multiple themes can be selected simultaneously to see overlapping influences
